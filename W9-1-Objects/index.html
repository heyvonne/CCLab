Scientific Name:

Agario

Discovered by / at:

Yvonne, March 15, 2025

Family:

NASA launched Project A, sending three astronauts to explore a mysterious planet. But everything went wrong when Chris’ spaceship malfunctioned, ejecting him into deep space. He called for help, but his two teammates abandoned him to save themselves. Drifting alone, he found a strange, glowing substance floating in the void. Desperate, he consumed it—and felt his body begin to change. He was growing, evolving into something beyond human. Now, he had a choice: Consume all and let his old friends die, or stay silent, vanish into the void, and take the secret of his transformation with him.

Habitat:

The Agario lives in the cold, dark emptiness of space, floating between the stars. It survives by eating anything it can find, growing bigger as it consumes energy and matter.

Appearance:

The creature was once human, but after consuming the glowing substance, the Agario's greed for more drives it to grow larger, changing color and size with each consumption. Intrigued by these changes, it becomes determined to keep searching for more substances to feed on.

Key Characteristics:

  • Floats in the stars
  • Eats glowy substances/squares
  • Is the feeling to kill or save his astronaut friends human or the creature's choice? Who knows.

What we know so far about [ Agario]? The agario was once human, but after eating a glowing substance in space, it starts to change. First, its hands and arms grow, then its whole body gets bigger. Each time it eats, its color and size change, and it becomes more driven to find more to consume. The creature’s hunger keeps growing, making it unpredictable and dangerous.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Humans should avoid approaching or interacting with the creature, as it becomes more dangerous with each substance it consumes. Keeping a safe distance and not offering any energy or matter is crucial to prevent triggering its hunger and aggression.
